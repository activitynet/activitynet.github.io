International Challenge on Activity Recognition (ActivityNet)

CVPR 2022 Workshop

Introduction

We are back for an in-person event!
The seventh installment of the annual International Challenge on Activity Recognition will be held in conjunction with CVPR22 on June 19, 2022, in New Orleans. Since the success of the previous ActivityNet Challenges ( 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021) and based on your feedback, we have worked hard on making this round richer and more inclusive. We are proud to announce that this year's challenge will be a pack full-day workshop with sessions discussing the outcome of many diverse challenges, which aim to push the limits of semantic visual understanding of videos.
